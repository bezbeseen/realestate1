The Story

During our first meeting we will

- Identify the specific streets we want to target
- Discuss and gather ideas on the desired style for the campaign
- Develop a promotional offer

We do the work

- We design the campaign
- You approve the design
- Print the piece that will then go to the mail

Get Them out

- We pay the postage
- USPS will deliver a card to every house in the selected routes

Get your customers attention

- The oversized postcard will stand out
- Brand recognition is increased
- The offer is read

Time for new Customers

- The Customer will scan the QR code
- Or better they will come in with the promotion in hand

Review and Plan

- After two weeks we report the analytics from the QR code
- Discuss the positives and negatives of the campaign
- Make adjustments and plan the next campaign
