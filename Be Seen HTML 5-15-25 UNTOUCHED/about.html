About

You're a small business owner looking to make a big impact in your local community. You want to stand out from the crowd, but you don't have the resources of those large national chains. That's where we come in. At Be Seen, we understand the power of print and design. We're not just another printing company - we're your local partner, here to help you make a lasting impression. Our key benefits are simple, yet essential. First, we offer convenience. No more dealing with impersonal online platforms or waiting for your order to arrive. With us, you can talk to a real person, and get your printing needs taken care of right away. We're right here in your neighborhood, ready to serve you.

Meet The Team

Team

Behzaad 'Bez' Morid

Founder

"The Vision"

"It all started with a pencil and paper in the back of the classroom, Learned how to transfer my art to digital with graphic design. Learned how to code my art on the web. Learned how to sell my art with a business."

A first-generation Iranian-American Bez was born in San Jose and embodies the spirit of entrepreneurship. His academic journey led him through DeAnza and UCSD, where he majored in Communication, honing the skills that would later become the foundation of his professional expertise. Bez took his passion for design and team building and navigated the intricate world of start-ups, culminating in the acquisition of the company he co-founded PayTollo by SiriusXM. Bez then transitioned into founding Be Seen LLC. where he applies his forward thinking ideas to all of his clients.

In addition to dedication to design, Bez is the visionary behind Living PMA (Positive Mental Attitude), a charitable organization aimed at funding juvenile brain cancer research. This endeavor is a heartfelt tribute to his Brother, inspiring a positive change in the lives of those affected by this devastating disease.

Marc Cadet

Partner

"The Operation"

"Through hard work and preparation, I have achieved great success in life. My experience in the printing industry has taught me the power of creating solutions for others. It has inspired me to expand my reach and help a larger number of people, realizing that my impact can extend far beyond a select few."

Meet Marc Cadet, a first-generation Haitian-American who journeyed from Poughkeepsie NY to California at the age of 9. Growing up in San Jose, Marc attended Andrew Hill High School and San Jose State University. Marc entered the printing industry where he gained the knowledge that he holds today.. 25+ years of providing printing and marketing solutions ensures an unparalleled experience to you.

Beyond boardrooms and the printing presses, he was a successful high school basketball coach for 15 years and a proud father of two young adults. Away from business Marc finds his zen in creating delectable culinary delights which he shares with his friends and family.
