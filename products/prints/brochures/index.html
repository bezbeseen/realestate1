Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a flyer and a brochure?

While flyers are typically flat, single-sheet handouts, brochures are folded and contain more detailed information, making them ideal for in-depth product or service descriptions.

What are the available folding options?

We offer several folding options, including the popular tri-fold, bi-fold (half-fold), and z-fold. We can also accommodate custom folding requests.

Can you help with the design of my brochure?

Yes, our experienced design team can help you create an effective and visually appealing brochure layout that communicates your message clearly.
