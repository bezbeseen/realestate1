Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of paper are the folders printed on?

We use thick, durable card stocks, typically 14pt or 16pt, to give your folders a sturdy, professional feel.

Can I have slits for a business card?

Yes, we offer standard business card slits on either the left or right pocket, or both. We can also add slits for CDs or brochures.

What is the capacity of the pockets?

Our standard 4-inch pockets can comfortably hold about 25 sheets of 20lb paper each.
