Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Lustre and Glossy finishes?

Lustre has a subtle sheen and a slight texture, which resists fingerprints and glare. Glossy has a highly reflective, smooth surface that makes colors appear very vibrant.

What file format should I upload my photos in?

For the best quality, we recommend uploading your photos as high-resolution JPEG (JPG) files in the sRGB color space.

Do you offer color correction?

We do not automatically color correct photos, as we aim to print your files as you have prepared them. Please ensure your monitor is calibrated for accurate color.
