Frequently Asked Questions

What material are the banners made from?

Our standard banners are made from a durable 13oz scrim vinyl. We also offer heavier 18oz vinyl for extra durability and 8oz mesh vinyl for windy outdoor conditions.

Are the banners suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, our vinyl banners are weatherproof and printed with UV-resistant inks, making them perfect for outdoor use. For very windy locations, we recommend our mesh banners.

How do I hang my banner?

Our banners come with grommets placed every 2-3 feet for easy hanging with rope, zip ties, or hooks. We also offer pole pockets as a finishing option.
