Frequently Asked Questions

Static cling is a non-adhesive material that sticks to glass through static electricity, perfect for temporary applications. Perforated window film has tiny holes that allow visibility from inside while maintaining a solid appearance from outside, ideal for long-term installations.

Static cling graphics typically last 6-12 months with proper care. Perforated window film can last 2-3 years or more. Both types are UV-resistant and designed for long-lasting performance.
