Why Choose Our Window Clings?

Window clings are the perfect solution for temporary promotions, sales, and branding on glass surfaces. Since they use static electricity instead of adhesive, they can be applied, removed, and reused multiple times without leaving any sticky residue.

Our high-quality printing process ensures your message is vibrant and professional, whether you choose a clear or white background. They are a versatile and budget-friendly option for any business.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do window clings stick to the window?

Window clings are made from a thin vinyl that uses static electricity to adhere to smooth, non-porous surfaces like glass. No adhesive is used, so they are easy to remove and reposition.

Can I reuse my window clings?

Yes! Simply peel the cling off the window, place it back on its original paper backing, and store it flat in a cool, dry place until you are ready to use it again.

What is the difference between inside-glass and outside-glass application?

Inside-glass clings are placed on the inside of the window to be viewed from the outside, which protects them from the elements. Outside-glass clings are applied directly to the exterior of the window.
