Why Choose an A-Frame Sign?

A-frame signs are one of the most effective and affordable ways to advertise on a sidewalk or at an event. Their portability allows you to place your message in high-traffic areas to draw customers directly to your door.

Whether you need a simple, sturdy sign for daily specials or a high-impact graphic for a special event, we have an A-frame solution that fits your needs and budget.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the signs weatherproof?

Yes, our A-frame signs and inserts are designed for outdoor use. The plastic Signicade models are particularly robust, and the printed inserts use weather-resistant inks and materials.

How do I change the signs on a metal A-frame?

The inserts on our metal A-frames simply slide in and out from the top, making it quick and easy to swap out your message.

How heavy are the signs?

Plastic Signicade models are lightweight (around 18-20 lbs) but can be filled with sand or water for extra stability in windy conditions. Metal frames are typically lighter.
