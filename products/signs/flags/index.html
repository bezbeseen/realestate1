Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between single-sided and double-sided flags?

Single-sided flags are printed on one side, and the design is visible in reverse on the back (bleed-through). Double-sided flags consist of two flags sewn together with a blockout liner in between, so the design reads correctly on both sides.

What kind of base should I choose?

A ground stake is best for soft surfaces like grass or soil. A cross base is ideal for hard, flat surfaces like concrete, both indoors and outdoors. A water bag can be added to the cross base for extra stability in windy conditions.

How do I care for my flag?

To prolong the life of your flag, we recommend bringing it indoors during severe weather. Flags can be machine washed on a gentle cycle with cold water and air-dried. Do not iron.
