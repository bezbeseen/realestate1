Why Choose 3D Lettering?

Dimensional lettering makes your business name or logo literally stand out. The shadows and depth created by 3D letters add a touch of class and sophistication that flat signs cannot replicate.

This versatile signage solution can be produced from a wide array of materials to perfectly match your brand aesthetic, from sleek brushed metal to colorful, glossy acrylic. Our expert team will help you choose the best material and mounting option for your space.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the letters made of?

We offer a huge selection of materials, including acrylic, PVC, foam, aluminum, stainless steel, and more. The best choice depends on your budget, where the sign will be installed (indoors or outdoors), and the look you want to achieve.

How are 3D letters installed?

Letters are typically stud-mounted. Studs are glued to the back of the letters, which are then inserted into holes drilled into the wall. This creates a secure, floating look. They can also be mounted flush or applied with high-strength adhesive tape.

Can these letters be illuminated?

This product category is for non-illuminated letters. If you are looking for illuminated 3D letters, please see our Channel Letters product page, which offers front-lit and halo-lit options.

Common Materials for 3D Lettering

Acrylic

Versatile and available in many colors. Can have a glossy, matte, or brushed metal finish. Great for both indoor and outdoor use.

Aluminum

Durable, lightweight, and weather-resistant. Can be finished with a brushed, polished, or painted look. Ideal for a sleek, modern aesthetic.

PVC

A lightweight and cost-effective plastic material. It is easy to fabricate and can be painted in any custom color. Best for indoor use.
