Why Choose a Light Box Sign?

Light box signs, or cabinet signs, are a classic and highly effective form of illuminated signage. They offer excellent visibility both day and night, making them a smart investment for any storefront or business.

We use modern, energy-efficient LED lighting that provides bright, consistent illumination while saving on electricity costs and requiring minimal maintenance compared to traditional fluorescent bulbs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the faces of the light box made from?

The sign faces are typically made from a translucent acrylic or polycarbonate material that is durable and allows for vibrant, even illumination.

Can I change the graphic on my light box?

Yes, one of the benefits of light box signs is that the graphic panels can be replaced. This makes it easy to update your branding or advertising without replacing the entire sign cabinet.

Are these signs difficult to install?

Light box signs require electrical wiring and should be installed by a qualified professional. We offer professional sign installation services to ensure your sign is mounted safely and correctly.
