Why Choose Yard Signs?

Yard signs are a powerful and budget-friendly tool for local advertising. They are perfect for grabbing the attention of foot and vehicle traffic in a specific neighborhood or location.

Made from durable corrugated plastic, our signs can withstand rain and sun. We print with vibrant, UV-cured inks to make sure your message is seen and remembered.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are H-stakes included with the signs?

H-stakes are not automatically included but can be easily added to your order. This allows you to purchase only the hardware you need.

How long will my yard signs last?

Our signs are durable and can last for over a year outdoors with proper care. To maximize their lifespan, bring them inside during extreme weather conditions like heavy wind or snow.

Can you print on both sides of the sign?

Yes, we offer both single-sided and double-sided printing options to ensure your message is visible from all directions.
