Why Choose Corrugated Signs?

Corrugated plastic signs are the go-to choice for affordable and durable temporary signage. Their fluted plastic construction makes them tough enough for outdoor use, yet lightweight and easy to transport and install.

We print directly onto the sign material with high-quality, UV-cured inks to ensure your design is vibrant and resistant to fading. Perfect for a wide range of applications where a temporary and economical sign is needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coroplast?

Coroplast is the brand name for a type of corrugated plastic, which is essentially a plastic version of cardboard. It is made of three layers with two flat sheets sandwiching a fluted, ribbed layer.

How long do these signs last outdoors?

With typical use, corrugated plastic signs can last for 1-2 years or more outdoors. While they are waterproof, prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and high winds can shorten their lifespan.

What are H-stakes?

H-stakes are H-shaped metal wires that are used to install corrugated signs in soft ground like lawns. The top prongs slide into the vertical flutes of the sign, and the bottom legs are pushed into the ground.
