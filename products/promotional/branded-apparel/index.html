Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the item and decoration method. Typically, screen printing has a minimum of 12 pieces, while embroidery can often be done for smaller quantities. Contact us for specifics.

Can I mix and match sizes?

Yes, you can mix and match apparel sizes within your order to outfit everyone on your team.

What file format do you need for my logo?

For the best results, we recommend providing your logo in a vector format (such as .AI, .EPS, or .SVG). If you do not have a vector file, a high-resolution raster file (.PNG, .PSD) may be acceptable.

Our Decoration Methods

Screen Printing

A cost-effective method for larger orders. Ink is applied to the garment through a mesh screen, creating a durable and vibrant print. Ideal for t-shirts and hoodies.

Embroidery

Your logo is stitched into the fabric, creating a high-end, professional look. Perfect for polos, jackets, and hats. Priced by stitch count.

Heat Transfer

Your design is printed on a special vinyl and then heat-pressed onto the garment. Great for complex, multi-color designs and smaller order quantities.
