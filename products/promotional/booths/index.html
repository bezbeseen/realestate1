Frequently Asked Questions

How portable are these displays?

Most of our systems are designed for portability. They break down into compact components and pack into durable carrying cases, many of which have wheels and can be checked as luggage for flights.

How long does it take to set up a booth?

Setup times vary by system. A simple pop-up backdrop can be set up by one person in minutes. More complex modular booths may take longer but are designed for straightforward assembly without the need for special tools.

Can I update the graphics on my display?

Yes, nearly all of our display systems allow for graphics to be easily replaced. This makes it cost-effective to update your branding or tailor your message for different events.

Our Display Systems

Pop-Up Displays

An accordion-style frame that expands to create a large backwall. Graphics are typically attached with velcro, making setup incredibly fast.

Fabric Tube Displays

A lightweight aluminum tube frame that snaps together. A "pillowcase" style fabric graphic slides over the frame for a seamless, taut finish.
