Why Use Promotional Pens?

Promotional pens are a proven marketing staple for a reason. They are affordable, practical, and get used daily, keeping your brand top-of-mind. Every time someone uses your pen, it is a reminder of your business.

Whether you need an inexpensive pen for a mass giveaway or a premium executive pen for a special client, we have the selection and expertise to deliver a product that represents your brand well.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order for custom pens?

Minimum order quantities depend on the specific pen model, but they typically start around 100-250 pieces.

What color ink do the pens have?

Most of our pens come standard with black ink. Blue ink is often available as an option. Please check the product details for the specific pen you are interested in.

Can you help me with the artwork for my pens?

Absolutely. Our design team can help you format your logo for the best possible imprint and can provide a digital proof for your approval before production begins.

Popular Pen Styles

Plastic Pens

The most budget-friendly option, available in thousands of color and style combinations. Perfect for large-scale giveaways.

Metal Pens

Offer a heavier, more premium feel. Ideal for corporate gifts and making a lasting impression. Often decorated with laser engraving.

Stylus Pens

A multi-function tool with a pen on one end and a soft stylus tip for touchscreens on the other. A modern and highly useful giveaway.
