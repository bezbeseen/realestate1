Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the tent?

Our tents use a pop-up frame that expands outwards. It is recommended to have two people for setup. Simply expand the frame, attach the canopy top, and raise the legs to the desired height.

Is the tent waterproof?

The canopy fabric is water-resistant and will protect from light rain. However, it is not designed to be completely waterproof in a heavy downpour.

How do I secure the tent?

The tent kit includes ropes and stakes for securing it on soft ground. For hard surfaces, we strongly recommend using weight bags on each leg to prevent it from moving in the wind.
